What is Khoj Kar ?

Khoj kar is service initiated by the Indian government. It helps in finding missing people. The government ties up with local services and uses face-recognition technologies to track missing individuals.

How to use the service ?

Go to the Regester Missing Person column. > Fill in the required details. > Upload a high quality picture of the missing person in jpg or png format. 80% of the face should be visible. Click submit and wait for the process. > You will receive a WhatsApp message once the missing person is traced by our system. Be patient and hope for the best.
